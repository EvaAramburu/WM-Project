Our Services

Our Vision

At WM, we assist entrepreneurial women with adopting digital solutions to make their businesses grow quantitative and qualitatively. Our commitment is to increase the digitization level of small businesses run by women, so they can take part in the digital market. We offer close and professional advice on fields in ever-increasing demand, such as digital marketing, e-commerce, or digital administration. Whether your company engages in selling physical or digital products and services, we have a solution for you! Rest easy, we will guide you every step of the way. We offer personalized advice and technical training, so the incorporation of new methods and tools will not hinder your business's general performance. We believe that helping women enter the digital market is a matter of equality and social responsibility. It means giving women a chance to reshape the market and the power to participate in the construction of a more equitable and supportive society. Let's do it together!

Our Team

We are a team of full-stack developers trying to make a change. Being deeply conscious of the gender gap in the IT industry, we decided to put our efforts into assisting and accompanying women who want to bring their bussiness to the digitial market. By doing so, we aim to turn the web into a safer, friendlier, and more egalitarian space for everyone.
